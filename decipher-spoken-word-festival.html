Decipher

Spoken Word Festival

Branding, AR, Typography

Decipher, a spoken word poetry festival celebrating the community of page poets, storytellers, songwriters and improvisers between the ages of 18-50 years old.

The typeface was inspired by the resourcefulness and improvisational qualities of redacted and fridge magnet poetry. Even though the novice word jester and poet laureate share the same words, it's in their combination that makes them skilled.

We know our audience loves to dig deeper into a poem for that ‚Äúaha!‚Äù moment. So we nested Decipher in a crowd of abstracted slam poetry scorecards as a nod to our discerning viewers. And like at the end of a performance with all eyes at the judges scorecards, they reveal the event details. This poster's mouthy shapes, unfussy grid, and jaunty colours encapsulates the spirit of our target audience.
