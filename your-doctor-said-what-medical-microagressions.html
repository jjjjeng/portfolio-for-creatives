Your Doctor Said What?!

Identifying Medical Microagressions

Branding, Interactive

The Truth and Reconciliation Commission's article #18 demands the implementation of healthcare rights free of discrimination to Indigenous peoples. In response, this campaign identifies medical microaggressions, explains their colonial legacies, and provides steps toward taking accountability.

Made in collaboration with Samantha Yeung.
